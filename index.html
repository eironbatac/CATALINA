About Us

Welcome to Catalina’s Homemade! Founded in 2019 in Barangay Imelda, Naval, Biliran, our business began with Catalina’s newfound love for baking and the abundance of fresh coconuts on our land. Today, each buko pie is made by hand, with only the freshest ingredients and a whole lot of heart.

Our pies are more than just a treat—they’re a slice of tradition. With every bite, you’ll taste the love and dedication that goes into each homemade creation. And to make it easier for you to enjoy, we offer free delivery right to your doorstep.

Buko-licious! Homemade, Heartmade. Come and taste the love!

To bake delicious, homemade buko pies using fresh, local ingredients that bring comfort and joy to every bite.
